The fact that other people get to decide what they think of your behavior leaves only two strategies for not suffering from those judgments: not doing anything, or not caring about the reaction. Not caring works surprisingly well.
—Clay Shirky, on confidently doing interesting things. (Via Put This On which has the full quote.)
